\# BizTrack — Small Business Control System  

\*\*Product Specification v1.0\*\*



---



\## 1. Overview



\### 1.1 Product Vision

BizTrack is a simple, practical business management system for small shop owners to control:

\- Stock

\- Sales \& purchases

\- Profit

\- Customer \& supplier dues

\- Cash flow



The goal is to replace notebooks and messy Excel sheets with a \*\*clear, real-time view of business health\*\* — without accounting complexity.



\### 1.2 Problem Statement

Small business owners:

\- Buy the same product at different prices over time

\- Sell at different prices to different customers

\- Rarely receive full payment instantly

\- Lose track of who owes them and whom they owe

\- Don’t know real profit (only sales)

\- Struggle with stock across shops/warehouses

\- Avoid complex accounting software



\### 1.3 Solution

BizTrack provides:

\- Simple purchase \& sales tracking

\- Automatic stock updates

\- Due (credit) management for customers and suppliers

\- Real profit calculation

\- Clear dashboards and reports in non-accountant language

\## 4. Key Features



\### 4.1 Product \& Inventory Management

\- Create / edit products

\- Track:

&nbsp; - SKU / Name

&nbsp; - Category

&nbsp; - Unit

&nbsp; - Stock quantity

\- Support:

&nbsp; - Multiple warehouses / locations

\- Low stock alerts

\- Stock movement history (in/out)



---



\### 4.2 Purchase Management (Buying)



\- Create purchase entries:

&nbsp; - Supplier

&nbsp; - Date

&nbsp; - Products \& quantities

&nbsp; - Unit cost

&nbsp; - Total amount

&nbsp; - Paid amount

&nbsp; - Due amount

\- Automatically:

&nbsp; - Increase stock

&nbsp; - Update average cost (or FIFO in future)

&nbsp; - Update supplier due

\- Purchase history per supplier

\- Edit / cancel purchase (with stock adjustment)



---



\### 4.3 Sales Management (Selling)



\- Create sales invoices:

&nbsp; - Customer

&nbsp; - Date

&nbsp; - Products \& quantities

&nbsp; - Selling price (flexible per sale)

&nbsp; - Total amount

&nbsp; - Paid amount

&nbsp; - Due amount

\- Automatically:

&nbsp; - Decrease stock

&nbsp; - Record revenue

&nbsp; - Update customer due

\- Print / PDF invoice

\- Sales history per customer



---



\### 4.4 Customer \& Supplier Management



\#### Customers:

\- Name, phone, address

\- Total purchases

\- Total paid

\- Current due

\- Payment history



\#### Suppliers:

\- Name, phone, address

\- Total purchases

\- Total paid

\- Current payable (due)

\- Payment history



---



\### 4.5 Due \& Payment Tracking (Core Differentiator)



\- Track:

&nbsp; - Who owes you money

&nbsp; - Whom you owe money

\- Record payments:

&nbsp; - Partial or full

&nbsp; - Against specific invoice or overall balance

\- Dashboard widgets:

&nbsp; - Total customer due

&nbsp; - Total supplier payable

&nbsp; - Overdue amounts

\- Future:

&nbsp; - SMS / WhatsApp / Email reminders



---



\### 4.6 Profit Calculation



\- Profit based on:

&nbsp; - Real purchase cost

&nbsp; - Actual selling price

\- Reports:

&nbsp; - Profit per invoice

&nbsp; - Profit per product

&nbsp; - Daily / monthly profit summary

\- Show:

&nbsp; - Revenue

&nbsp; - Cost

&nbsp; - Profit (clear separation)



---



\### 4.7 Reports \& Dashboard



\#### Dashboard:

\- Today’s sales

\- This month’s sales

\- This month’s profit

\- Total customer due

\- Total supplier payable

\- Low stock alerts



\#### Reports:

\- Sales report (date range)

\- Purchase report (date range)

\- Profit report

\- Stock report

\- Due report (customers \& suppliers)
